Summative Assessment

Lesson Plan

Central Question

In what ways did the Haudenosaunee persevere through and after the American Revolution?

Introduction

You will be assigned either one or both of the following summative tasks to demonstrate your learning by responding to the digital inquiry book’s central question: In what ways did the Haudenosaunee persevere through and after the American Revolution? 

Graphic Organizer

Complete the chart below using responses from the image collage, document analyses, video analysis, and chapter reviews from Chapters 1–5.

Summative Assessment
Graphic Organizer

Challenges faced by the Haudenosaunee as a result of the American Revolution  Acts of perseverance by the Haudenosaunee in response to the challenge  Supporting evidence of perseverance from Chapters 1–5 documents and videos 

Argumentative Paragraph

Using evidence from sources in Chapters 1–5, respond to the following question with an original argument:

Despite many challenges, in what ways did the Haudenosaunee persevere through the American Revolution?

After reading and analyzing several primary and secondary sources describing the effects of the Revolution on the Haudenosaunee, write a paragraph that responds to the question. Support your position with evidence from the sources.
